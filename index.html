Featured Engagement

Amazon Business Intelligence Engineering Project

Migration of a seller-support investigation tool from legacy systems to modern AWS-based analytics infrastructure.

Challenge

The legacy Top Offender UI relied on DynamoDB, S3 and DJS jobs, resulting in slow dashboard loads and limited scalability across multiple violation types.

Solution

Built a new analytics pipeline using Matching data lake sources, Andes, S3, Cradle, Glue, Athena and QuickSight with customer-level filtering.

Outcome

  • 100x faster dashboard load times
  • 100% UAT success with Seller Support
  • Billions of records processed
  • Latency reduced from minutes to seconds
